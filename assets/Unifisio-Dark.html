Unifisio Dark mode first

Design Token System

A dark-mode-first palette for a kinesiology & physiotherapy clinic. Deep ocean navy surfaces, cyan that reads like light through water, and glows in place of drop shadows. Every value is production-ready and audited for WCAG AA.

01 — The reasoning

Two brand colors, on deep navy

#0B5E8A
Logo blue

On #07111D it lands near 2.0:1 — barely separable from the background. Unusable for text or buttons.

→ Demoted to ambient glow only
#00C9E4
Brand cyan

On #07111D it hits 9.5:1 — it glows against the deep navy and stays crisp at small sizes.

→ Promoted to PRIMARY accent

09 — Verification

Contrast audit · WCAG AA

Ratios computed from relative luminance. AA needs ≥4.5:1 for body text, ≥3:1 for large text & UI.

Foreground Background Ratio Result
text #DCF0F8bg #07111D16.1:1AAA
text #DCF0F8surface #0C1B2B14.8:1AAA
primary #00C9E4bg #07111D9.5:1AAA
primary #00C9E4surface #0C1B2B8.7:1AAA
text-muted #6B9BB5surface #0C1B2B5.8:1AA
gold #F59E0Bsurface #0C1B2B8.1:1AAA
success #34D399surface #0C1B2B9.0:1AAA
white #FFFFFFprimary #00C9E42.0:1FAIL
→ fix: bg #07111Dprimary #00C9E49.5:1AAA
⚠
The brief's white button text fails AA — pure white on the bright cyan lands at 2.0:1. Primary buttons must use dark ink (--color-bg #07111D, 9.5:1) on the cyan fill. This is reflected in the demo button below.
✓ dark ink, 9.5:1
